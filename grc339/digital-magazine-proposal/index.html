Introduction

Solstice

As the sun drapes itself over terracotta rooftops and sea-kissed shores, Solstice invites you to wander through a season of effortless beauty and quiet indulgence. This is your passport to Europe’s golden hours, from linen-draped mornings in Lisbon to sunset aperitivos along the Amalfi Coast. Let this issue be your sun-drenched companion, a soft breeze through a European summer well lived.
