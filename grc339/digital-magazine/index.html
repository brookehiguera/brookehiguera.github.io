Italy

From sun-drenched Amalfi coastlines to pasta-fueled piazza nights in Rome, Italy is a summer love affair waiting to happen. Wander through Tuscan vineyards, get lost in Venice’s winding canals, or dive into the turquoise waters of Sardinia. Sip limoncello at golden hour, savor every bite of handmade pasta, and embrace the effortless charm of la dolce vita — where every moment feels like a scene from a classic film.

Portugal

Golden beaches, tiled streets, and the soulful sounds of fado — Portugal is where ancient charm meets coastal cool. Whether you're surfing in Ericeira or wine-tasting in the Douro Valley, it's pure magic.

Croatia

With sapphire waters and medieval towns, Croatia is a summer stunner. Island hop through the Adriatic or wander Dubrovnik’s old walls — every moment is postcard-worthy.

Spain

Late-night tapas, flamenco rhythms, and sun-soaked plazas — Spain is a celebration of life. From Barcelona’s bold design to Andalusia’s sultry heat, every region tells a story.

Germany

Germany in summer is a mix of lakeside serenity and vibrant city escapes. Explore Berlin’s creative pulse, hike Bavaria’s lush trails, or cruise the Rhine — all under endless blue skies.

Netherlands

Biking along canals, lounging in tulip fields, or beach days on the North Sea coast — the Netherlands is more. Summer brings a breezy, effortless charm to this progressive gem.

Greece

Whitewashed villages, cerulean seas, and fresh grilled seafood — Greece is a dreamy escape. Whether you're dancing on Mykonos or chasing history in Athens, it’s endlessly captivating.

France

From lavender fields in Provence to Riviera chic in Nice, France defines summer elegance. Sip rosé under the sun, get lost in Parisian side streets, and fall in love with every detail.

Montenegro

Still a hidden gem, Montenegro dazzles with dramatic fjords, medieval towns, and Adriatic charm. Think Croatia’s little sister — wild, beautiful, and quietly luxurious.

Denmark

Cool, calm, and design-forward — Denmark is the home of hygge summers. Wander Copenhagen’s harbor, island-hop by bike, or enjoy long, golden Nordic evenings.

Czechia

Storybook towns and sun-drenched beer gardens make Czechia a summer surprise. Prague stuns year-round, but its cobbled charm truly sparkles in the sunshine.

Slovenia

Green, serene, and surprisingly under-the-radar — Slovenia is where the Alps meet the Adriatic. Kayak on Lake Bled, sip wine in the Soča Valley, and embrace the great outdoors.
